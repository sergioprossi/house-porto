The Apartment

A 2-bedroom apartment on the 1st floor of an independent building on Rua do Breiner, in the heart of Cedofeita.

The building dates from before 1951 but was completely rebuilt in 2025 — which means the charm and character of the original structure are preserved, while the bones of the building (structure, plumbing, electrics) are brand new.

Inside, the apartment has a vintage warmth that feels authentic and inviting, not sterile. Original details — think high ceilings, wood floors, Portuguese tilework — sit alongside a modern open-plan kitchen that connects to the living and dining area.

Natural light fills the space throughout the day.

The Neighbourhood

Cedofeita is where Porto residents actually live — and one of the most sought-after parishes in the city.

Rua do Breiner sits in the bohemian quarter, steps from Rua Miguel Bombarda and its galleries, surrounded by independent cafes, local restaurants, and neighbourhood shops. It's central enough to walk everywhere, yet quiet enough to sleep with the windows open.

This isn't a tourist zone. This is a real neighbourhood with real community — and a growing number of international residents who chose it for exactly that reason.
